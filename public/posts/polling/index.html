What is Wrong With Polling Station Facilities?

December 15, 2019 · Brittany Stenekes

We recognize that long waiting times at a voting poll dissuades people from voting. In addition, if sacrificing an hourly wage is required to afford time at the ballot box, minority populations, and low income populations may be more severly effective.

The objective of this paper is to explore various methods that can reduce the voter waiting time outside of voting centers My team hopes our insights could improve the voting experience and promote democratic election results.
